ABOUT US

After more than 35 years in the industry, Hen Lin has developed into the reputable name that it is today. Hen Lin has continued to be inventive, though, as evidenced by its continuous investment in cutting-edge technologies to significantly raise the caliber and consistency of its output. Hen Lin has adopted the HAACP (Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Point) and GMP (Good Manufacturing Practices) protocols to guarantee the highest caliber dim sum products available to our customers.
