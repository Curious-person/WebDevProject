Frequently Asked Questions

      Sometimes, we cannot answer all your queries immediately, in order to quickly get a response, we have compiled questions normally asked us and we try to answer them as best we can. However, if you still need to ask us questions not stated here, you may use the contact forms under the Contact Us option.

What is special about Hen Lin products?

      Hen Lin has been producing good quality products for over 35 years now. Although, we are known for our dimsum and noodles, our product line is quite extensive including products like Lechon Macau, Quekiam, Braised Beef, Lomi, etc.

Are Hen Lin products safe?

      Yes, our products undergo testing in our own laboratory to reduce or minimize the incidence of hazards. Hen Lin is following the rigorous procedures as required by the HACCP standard. All products are examined and pass through metal detectors to provide additional safeguards against hazards.

What is H.A.C.C.P?

      Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points (HACCP) is a systematic preventive approach to food safety, pharmaceutical safety, etc. that addresses physical, chemical and biological hazards as a means of prevention rather than finished product inspection. (Courtesy: Wikipedia)
